BOOKING TERMS AND CONDITIONS.



  • Booking:A minimum deposit of 50% of the total hire price is required at the time of booking with the remaining balance to be paid no later than 7 days before the event. Payments can be made by banks transfer or alternately payments can be made by credit or debit cards over the phone.

  • Cancellations: In the case of cancellations all deposits or payments in full are fully transferrable up to 7 days prior to the event (subject to availability on any new date) but not refundable.

  • Any cancellations made within 7 days before the event are not transferrable or refundable as this equipment has been reserved solely for the client and is impossible to rehire at this late stage.

  • With regards to cancellations due to the weather we would like to make the client aware that if an event is cancelled for this reason it is still subject to the same cancellation policy as above.

  • This is because many of our clients and otential clients have indoor options and facilities available for their event to go ahead under any weather conditions or circumstances and king bouncealot cannot be held responsible for any losses due to the weather.

  • Risk Assessment Section:

    I should make all users awareof these instructions. Problems or faults must be reported to King Bounce-Alot. Under no circumstance should repairs be attempted.

  • Children's equipment is not safe and is not designed to be used by adults. Adult supervision must be kept at all times when the equipment is in use. Any pegs and ropes must stay in place and must be replaced if any do become loose.

  • Wall , roiling the equipment, somersaults or climbing walls are not allowed.

  • Food, drinks, shoes, and any sharp objects are not allowed.

  • Scarves and necklaces should not be worn.

  • No one should be allowed to play with/ around the blower.
